BIOGRAPHY

The Orvcle aka BOMBO is undeniably one of the most unorthodox and intriguing musicians in the world. His style of music and pen game is unparalleled, with catchy rhythms and rhymes that set him apart from the rest. Bombo refuses to be confined to any category, instead carving out his own path in the industry.

The Orvcle fearlessly and flawlessly blends humors, slangs and life experiences into his lyrics, making him original and a true standout. He has effortlessly surpassed the line of upcoming rappers, as he refuses to imitate or follow trends. Instead, he has created a lane of his own, which has the streets talking about him.

"Lepezi" (his recent single) is the song that got people asking "who is this guy", and it remains one of the best rap songs in the industry today. Thanks to his growing and dedicated fanbase, Bombo has gone from being a battle rapper from his university days to a full-time Afro-fusion artist and songwriter on a consistent journey to stardom.
